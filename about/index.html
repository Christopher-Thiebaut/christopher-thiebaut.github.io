About

Hi, my name is Chris Thiebaut. I'm an iOS developer from Michigan. Currently, I work for Ford Motor Company developing the Ford Pass and Lincoln Way iOS Apps. While I'm very proud of my work on those apps, you won't see any posts about them here due to the proprietary nature of that code. In my spare time, I'm always looking to improve myself and my skills. Lately, I'm especially interested in improving understanding of how to structure object oriented projects so that the complexity scales well over time and the code I deliver can provide value for years to come. I'm also very interested in Test Driven Development and always interested in learning more about how to write tests that deliver the maximum safety and ability to continually refactor and improve code. I'm also a Swift language nerd. (Property wrappers are my favorite recent feature, but I'm struggling with how to best use them to deal with cross-cutting concerns without comprimising structure and testability. Expect a post on here if I ever find a solution I'm satisfied with on that.) I'm also always interested in learning more about how to deliver great apps for Apple platforms.
